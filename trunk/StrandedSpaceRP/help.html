Chat Commands

/help - Show this help.

//<message> - Talk in OCC.
/w <message> - Whisper.
/y <message> - Yell.
/pm <player> <message> - Send a PM.

/citizen - Become a citizen.
/cop - Become a cop.
/gangster - Become a gangster.
/doctor - Become a doctor.
/weaponsmith - Become a weaponsmith.
/mayor - Become the Mayor.
/mobboss - Become the Mob Boss.

/title <title> - Set the title to a door.
/addowner <player> - Add an owner to a door.
/removeowner <player> - Remove an owner from a door.
/owners - List a door's owners.

/dropmoney <ammount> - Drop money.
/givemoney <ammount> - Give money to the player you are looking at.

/warrant <player> - Issue a warrant on a player.
/unwarrant <player> - Unwarrant a player.
/warrants - List active warrants.
/hit - If you are the Mayor or Mob Boss, you can issue a hit on the Mayor or Mob Boss.

/printmoney <ammount> - Print money. (Admin Only)
/makeresource <resource> <ammount> - Create resources. (Admin Only)
/unownable - Make a door unownable. (Admin Only)

Needs

You need food, water and sleep to survive. You can track all three stats, as well as your health,
by looking at the HUD box in the top left corner.

Health

Food

Water

Sleep

If you lack any stat below zero, you will lose health, and eventually die. To get food, simply press use on anything edible. It will instantly increase
your food gauge, and also a little health. To get water, stand in a body of water and press use.
To sleep, use the sleep command from the F3 menu. Make sure you are in a shelter before going to sleep!
